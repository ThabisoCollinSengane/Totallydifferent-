Totally Different

Totally Different was born in Durban in 2024, out of a simple frustration: too much of what we wore — and what we put in our hair — felt exactly the same. So we set out to make the opposite. Every piece in our range, from heavyweight tees and utility outerwear to our curl creams and scalp oils, is designed and tested right here at home, for our climate and our people. Premium materials, honest pricing, and a fit that actually feels like you. Not louder — just totally different.
